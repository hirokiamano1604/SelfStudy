## 1. 新パターン：間接疑問文の語順（今日の最重要学習点）

2箇所で同じミスが出ている：

| 元の文 | 問題 | 修正 |
|---|---|---|
| how **is** the society **structured** | 疑問文の語順のまま | how society **is** structured |
| what **is** he really **want** to do | 同上 + SVA | what he really **wants** to do |

**ルール**: 間接疑問文（文の中に埋め込まれた疑問）は **平叙文の語順** になる。

```
直接疑問文: How is society structured?    （疑問文語順: is + 主語）
間接疑問文: ...aware of how society is structured.  （平叙文語順: 主語 + is）

直接疑問文: What does he want?
間接疑問文: ...conscious of what he wants.
```

**見分け方**: 文の先頭に "How" "What" が来たら疑問文語順。文の途中に how/what が来たら平叙文語順。

Introduction でも同じミス：
> what a type of person **is** he/she → what type of person **he or she is**

## 9. 書き直し例（Introduction + Body 2）

### Introduction

> Nowadays, it is increasingly common for young people to take a gap year between completing secondary school and entering university. Whether this is a beneficial decision depends largely on the individual's circumstances and prior experiences.

ポイント: 2文。間接疑問文の語順が正しい。"depends largely on" でグラデーション。

### Body 2

> Whether a gap year is beneficial depends on the individual. Students who have already gained practical experience through volunteer work or extracurricular research may find little additional value in delaying their studies. Conversely, for those whose exposure has been limited to formal education, a gap year can serve as a valuable opportunity to explore potential career paths and develop a clearer sense of direction. Ultimately, the decision should be based on whether the individual stands to gain experiences that their existing education has not provided.

ポイント: whether の正しい使い方、"Conversely" で対比、最後にLink文で問題に戻す。
