### Introduction（問題文の言い換え）
The pie charts in the picture illustrate the percentages of four different sources of news for 2010 and 2020.



### Overview（全体傾向 — 最重要パラグラフ）
The sectors of online and other rose, while TV, newspaper and radio sources declined significantly over the period.


### Detail 1
First of all, the share of online and other decreased sharply in 2020 compared to that of 2010.
In 2010, the percentage of online media was 10%, however, the figure increased to 50% which is five times as the former number.
As for "other" sector, having 5% share in 2010, it slightly rose to 7% in 2020.
