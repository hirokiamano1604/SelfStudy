### Introduction
The pie charts in the picture illustrates the percentages of five main sources of news for 2010 and 2020.


### Overview
Roughly, the sectors of online and other increased during the period, while the share of TV, newspaper and radio sources declined sharply in 2020 compared to 2010.


### Detail 1（↑ Online, Other）
First of all, the percentages of online and other increased over the period.
In 2010, the shere of online media was 10%. Conversely, the figure rose markedly and reached 50% which was the largest across the five main sources in 2020.
As for the sector of other, the percentage slightly increased from 5% in 2010 to 7% in 2020.



### Detail 2（↓ TV, Newspaper, Radio）
On the other hand, the ratio of TV, newspaper and radio declined sharply after the 10 years.
The proportion of TV was 40% in 2010, which was a dominant figure over the five sectors, whereas, in 2020, it reached 25%.
Additionally, having 30% share in 2010, the percentage of newspaper dropped to 10%.
