## Essay（20分 — 280語を目安に止める）

### Introduction（2文: 背景の言い換え + 立場）
People in some countries are encouraged by governments or their employers to have a short and regular rest while they are working, such as walking outside for a short time or stretching.
I think the advantages of the rest covers the disadvantages because it is better for people in terms of working efficiency and their health.

### Body Paragraph 1（Advantages）
Taking rest during people's working time has advantages for their efficiency and health in a long term.
In general, if people work without taking any rests, they will gradually lose the energy to think carefully.
In addtion, while they are working, they are unlikely to stand but to sit down for long time, which is not appropriate for maintaining better health.
By having a short walk or stretching, they can partially recover from being tired and having less energy to think, and additionaly the circulation of blood in their legs will be promoted, which leads to their better conditions of their legs.
Therefore, having a short time rest benefits people from these kinds of aspects.


### Body Paragraph 2（Disadvantages）
On the other hand, there are some disadvantages for the encouragement in the viewpoints of time efficiency and concentration.
Although I raised that the pause improve the working efficiency, it is inevitable to spend at least a few minites of their labor time.(労働時間)
Furthermore, they need to quit their concentration while they are away from working.
For example, in order to walk around office for a short time, worker has to stop engaging in the stuff, and this pause may disturb thier concentration.
Hence, the benefit of taking a rest may conversely become disadvantages, in fact.




### Conclusion（新情報を入れない。要約 + 立場の再確認）
In conclusion, I think taking a rest will benefit people more then the disadvantages it has.
It is true that the method may disturb people's working. However, I think the positive influence of the time overwhelms the disadvantages.
Overall, the encouragement enable people to improve their outcomes in the long term, if we consider of the health or conditions of the people.
