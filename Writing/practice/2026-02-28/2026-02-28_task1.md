The figure of riders (in million) for different three types of public transportation in 2010 and 2020 is shown in the table.



### Overview（全体傾向 — 最重要パラグラフ）
Overall, the sum of the number of passenger for all types of transportation rose significantly.
Especially, the number for train and tram increased sharply while usage of bus decreased steadily.



### Detail 1
First of all, the total of passengers of train and bus in 2020 was 1.5 times as that in 2010, and the figure by large increased too.
In 2010, the figure of riders for train was 12 million, which is the largest among all transportations. 
Further more, it reached 18 million in 2020, having a highest share of usage.
As for tram, the number of user increased from 6 million in 2010 to 9 million in 2020, which incremented 1.5 times.
Although tram was the least popular way transport in 2010, it got out of the place in 2020.
In addition, the sum of the passengers of all three kinds of transoprtations was 32 in 2020, rising from 26 in 2010.
