## Essay（20分 — 280語を目安に止める）

### Introduction（2文: 背景の言い換え + 原因・解決の両方に触れる）
Nowadays, a lot of cities are suffering trafic jam. This phenomenon is caused because of problems of time and space.
The possible solutions to reduce the harm of the conjunction would be a diversification of timing and pathaway.


### Body Paragraph 1（Causes）
The reasons of trafic jam are divided into two aspect: time and space.
One reason is that too many people use car in a certain period of a day, making car in the load stuck.
Since the amount of car exceeds the capacity of the load, the cars cannot go smoothly and get stuck.
For example, people tend to use car around 8 a.m. to 9 a.m. to commute their office, which will cause trafic jam around the period.
In addition, the narrowness of load is another reason which leads to heavier trafic conjunction.
Around a certain area with a narrow pathaway, cars are likely to be stuck because they have to decrease their speeds.
These backgrounds are factors of traffic jam in general.



### Body Paragraph 2（Solutions）
On the other hand, some solutions can be raised based on the two aspects, time and space.
A solution for the issue of concentration is to diversify people's moving time.
Promoting the flex time system for office in the city or prohibiting people's usage of certain loads based on the car number and time, which is the way China utilize, may be effective.
Additionally, as for the issue of space, we can apply AI in order to optimize the amount of traffic, considering a capacity of the load.
Although it will be difficult to expand the width of loads from now, in the future, we will be able to depend on AI while we are heading for a location in car.
In the situation, AI will choose the way to go to destination. 
By merging the information of peolpe's way, they can increase or decrease the amount of cars by sequentially chaging the order, and make the traffic smooth.




### Conclusion（新情報を入れない。要約 + まとめ）
In conclusion, the factors of trafic jam in many cities are the concentation of timing for people and the capacity of loads.
In order to tackle with this, the solution such as organizing the ability to pass through the load or optimizing people's ways may be effective.
