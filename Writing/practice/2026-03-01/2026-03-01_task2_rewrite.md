## 問題文（リライト用）

> In many cities, traffic congestion is a serious problem.
>
> **What do you think are the causes of this? What measures could be taken to reduce it?**

**フォーマット**: Intro（予告だけ）→ Body 1（原因）→ Body 2（解決策）→ Conclusion（Body のキーワードで要約、新情報なし）

## リライト（20分）

### Introduction（2文: 背景の言い換え + これから原因・対策を論じる予告のみ）
Nowdays, a lot of cities are suffering traffic jam and need to tackle this serious issue.
This essay will discuss causes of this problem and suggest possible solutions which can be utilized.
(文章が短くなってしまうと不安になる。けど、冗長にすることは正しくないよね)

### Body Paragraph 1（Causes）
The reasons of this issue are concentration on certain time periods and road, which leads to exceed the capacity of the road.
People use car to commute in general, and the time and routes people usually drive are decided in many cases.
Around the commuting time such as 8 a.m. or 9 a.m., a lot of cars use certain roads which are easier for people to arrive at their destination.
In another case, some roads are narrow enough to prevent the smoothness of the transportation, which can be treated as a spacial problem.
Therefore, these factors make cars to decrease their speeds, which cause traffic jam in many cities.
15:20



### Body Paragraph 2（Solutions）
To tackle this issue, the possible measures are to diverse the commuting time or the routes which majority of cars use.
The one solution is to promoting companies to introduce the flex time system or removing core time system.
By carrying out this, people will have options to go to office in noon or early morning, and the concentration of using cars on certain time periods will be eased.
In addition, in order to tackle the spacial problem, we can utilize the technology of AI for diversifying people's routes.
In the future, the automation of driving is goning to develop, and AI decides the routes to go.
By gathering the information of traffic and controlling the decision of AI, it will be possible to induce people's routes not to concentrate on the roads.
These solution would be effective in order to solve the traffic issue.




### Conclusion（Body のキーワードで要約。新情報を入れない）
In conclusion, traffic jam in many cities are caused by too much concentration on the certain time periods and the certain roads.
The problem can be eased by diversifying the commuting time and routes with the promotion of flex time system or the AI based traffic controll.
