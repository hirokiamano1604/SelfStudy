## Essay（20分 — 280語を目安に止める）

### Introduction（2文: 背景の言い換え + 両方の見解と自分の立場の予告）
Nowadays, people claim that minority languages should be preserved by governments by allocating budget, but critics state that the way of using budget is a waste of money and the minority speaker should acquire the majority language. In balance, I think we should spend decent portion of budget to protect the minority languages.


### Body Paragraph 1
People believe that we should put effort to preserve minority languages, because any languages can be recovered once it extincts.
Languages reflect the way of thinking of the speakers and how do the speakers perceive the things around them, in terms of cultural anthoropology.
This, in turn, means that once a language extincts without any protection, it can be said that the culture of the language has lost and it's reviving would be hard.  For example, a language in Ireland is about to disappear and be replaced by English, which means that the way of thinking for Ireland language speakers is likely to be replaced by that of English speaker. Preservation of the language is not only the protection of knowledge but also clues of the culture.

### Body Paragraph 2
On the other hand, some critics state that the protection is wrong way of using budget of their governments, since protecting language doesn't improve their daily lives directly. Only focusing on protecting the knowledge and culture of minority people, the measures are carried out for certain people. Since the budget of the government and the usage are limited, people still demand additional helps with an investment on the sector which directly connects with thier daily lives. Therefore, people wish to focus on the other measures instead of the language preservation. For exmaple, if person is suffering a lack of money needed for fostering his child, he will think that spending money on the minority language should be alternated by the measures to help parents including him.
Hence, some people want their governement to focus on the policies close to their daily lives, not the language protection.



### Conclusion（新情報を入れない。要約 + 立場の再確認）
Overall, although there are positive and negative aspects of preserving the minor languages, I think we should keep protecting the minor languages. 
In the long term, the loss of culture is not possible to recover from scratch, and speakers' perception should be conserved somehow.

### 11. 振り返りの考え方（TR/CC ↑ なのに GRA/LR ↓ の整理）

**起きていること**  
「長く書きすぎた → 時間オーバー → 見直し時間が足りない → 文法・凡ミスが増えた」という**時間の取り合い**が起きている。

**悪い振り返り**  
- 「長く書いたから悪い」→ 違う。**論点が足りているから TR/CC は伸びている**。  
- 「次は短く書こう」だけ→ 短くしすぎると今度は TR（展開不足）や CC（流れの薄さ）が落ちる。

**良い振り返り**  
- **論点の数はそのまま、語数だけ削る**。「言い換えの重複」と「1文で足りることを2文にしている部分」を削る。  
- そうすると **280語前後で同じ内容** が書ける → 時間に余裕 → **最後の5分で冠詞・スペル・間接疑問文を見直す** → GRA/LR が落ちない。  
- まとめ: **「多く書く」のではなく「無駄を削る」**。論の質（TR/CC）は維持しつつ、冗長な部分だけ短縮して時間を確保し、見直しで凡ミスを防ぐ。
