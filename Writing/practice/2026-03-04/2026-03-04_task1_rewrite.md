## リライト（17分）

### Introduction
The bar chart in the picture represents the percentages of households with internet access in countries A, B and C in 2010 and 2020.


### Overview
Overall, the proportions increased significantly among these three countires, but the extent of increase varied.


### Detail 1
First of all, the figures of country A and C roughly doubled over the 10 years.
The share in country was 42% in 2010, but in 2020, it reached 81%, keeping the percentage in the second place over the period.
As for country C, starting from 28% in 2010, the proportion ended up with 64%. 
Although the figure was the smallest thoughout 10 years, the extent of the increase was more significent compared to the others.



### Detail 2
On the other hand, the growth of the percentage in country C was slightly less than the others.
In 2010, the share was 58%, which was already the largest value among three countries.
After the 10 years, the share finally became 85% with an increase by 27%.
Even though the figure was in the first place in both of the years, the extent of rise was about a half of the former value, which was less than the other countries.

### 総評
**初回から改善した点**: Overview で **the extent of increase varied** が使えている。Detail 2 で C を 58%→85% と正しく書けている。**increased by 27%** は「27 percentage points」と書くとより明確（下記）。
**最優先で直す点**: **Detail 1 で B と C が入れ替わっている**。28%→64% は **Country B**、58%→85% は **Country C**。Detail 1 は「A・B」、Detail 2 は「C」なので、「As for country C, starting from 28%」→ **Country B**（28→64）に修正する。あわせてスペル（countires, thoughout, significent）と「The share in country」→ Country A。
