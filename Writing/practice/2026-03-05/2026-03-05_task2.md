## Essay（20分 — 280語を目安に止める）

### Introduction（2文: 背景の言い換え + 立場）
Nowadays, some people claim that free childcare for working parents should be provided by the governments, while the others state that the fee should be paid by themselves. On balance, I agree with the first opinion, which encourage the governments to allocate budget for free childcare.

### Body Paragraph 1
First of all, people want their governments to be responsible for childcare especially for working parents. 
Since working parents are relatively busy, they are unlikely to have enough time to take care of their child.
Hence, they have to use childcare center, which requires them to pay the fee.
Bearing the burden instead of the parents, the governments can promote people to give a birth in the long run.
Helping working parents would benefit people and the local in the future.
14分




### Body Paragraph 2
On the other hand, the other people suggest the working parents to pay the childcare fee by themselves.
If both of them work, they theoretically earn twice as the other families in which either father or mother works.
Since the budget of the governments is limited, people who criticize propose to allocate the budget to the other fields such as for elderly or education.
For example, the cancellation of the free childcare policy may enable an investment for cleaning up the local high school to be carried out.
Supporting working parents is not necessarily the best way to improve the local economy.
22分半



### Conclusion（新情報を入れない。要約 + 立場の再確認）
In conclusion, positive reactions for the free childcare policy are based on the perspective that the working parents simply require the financial aid, while the opposite opinion is that there may be better way of using the budget of the government. On balance, I think the government should initially help people in need, vene if there may be other ways to improve the local environment.

### 8. government の単数・複数（メモへの回答）
- **the government**（単数）= 一国の政府を指すとき。動詞は単数（the government should）。
- **governments**（複数・無冠詞）= 政府一般・複数国を漠然と指すとき。
- **the governments** = 特定の複数の政府（文脈で「その各国政府」と分かるとき）。このエッセイでは **the government** で統一すると自然。

3. **コロケーション** — give birth（無冠詞）、reaction **to**、investment **in**。書く前に不安な語は word-bank で確認。
